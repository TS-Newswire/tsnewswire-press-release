# SaaS Press Release Template

> For product launches, funding, major features, and partnerships. Lead with
> the customer outcome, not the feature list. Headline ≤ 110 characters.
> Quantify wherever possible (customers, % improvement, time saved).

---

**FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE**

# [Company] Launches [Product/Feature] to Help [Audience] [Achieve Outcome]

**[CITY, Country] — [Month DD, YYYY]** — [Company name], the [category, e.g.
"workflow automation platform"], today launched [product/feature], [one
sentence on the outcome it delivers].

## The news

[2–3 sentences: what's available today, who it's for, and the headline
capability. Include one proof point — a metric, a design partner result, or
adoption number.]

## Why it matters

[2–3 sentences on the problem and the measurable improvement. Reference the
status quo it replaces and the cost/time it removes.]

> "[Quote from a named executive — the 'why now']," said [Full Name], [Title]
> at [Company]. "[Second sentence — customer impact or vision.]"

## Customer proof (optional but powerful)

> "[Quote from a named customer with their company and result]," said [Name],
> [Title] at [Customer Company].

## Availability & pricing

[GA date, regions, plans/tiers, free trial, and a link to sign up or docs.]

## About [Company]

[2–3 sentence boilerplate: what the company does, funding/backers if notable,
and the website URL.]

### JSON-LD checklist for this release

- `articleSection`: `"Technology"` or `"SaaS"`
- `keywords`: e.g. `["saas", "<category>", "<integration>", "b2b software"]`
- Include a product screenshot or logo lockup as the `image`.
- Generate markup with `node src/cli.js your-release.json --tag`.
