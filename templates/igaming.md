# iGaming Press Release Template

> For casino, sportsbook, and gaming-supplier news. Keep claims licensable and
> jurisdiction-accurate — name the regulator and license number where relevant.
> Headline ≤ 110 characters. Avoid promotional/bonus language in the body; that
> belongs on marketing pages, not in a press release (and many outlets reject it).

---
